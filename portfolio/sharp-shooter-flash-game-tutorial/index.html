  • Game Name: Sharp Shooter
  • Version: 1.00
  • Type: One Player
  • Controls: Mouse click (shoot)
  • Development Platform: Flash CS4
  • Programming Language: Action Script 3.00 (AS3.0)
  • Description: Some tank or shooting machine shooting at some enemies trying to approach it. Top view game where player controls the Gun direction of tank with movements of mouse and click to shoot the approaching enemies. When an enemy hits our player Tank, it would destroy player Tank.
